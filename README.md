# Yaesu FT-8XX Suite by K3LH

## What Is This?

The Yaesu FT-8XX Suite is a free, all-in-one radio control and operating application for the Yaesu FT-817, FT-818, FT-857 and FT-897 series of radios, developed by K3LH.

---

## Why Does This Exist?

The Yaesu FT-8XX line is a beloved series of radios that has stood the test of time in the amateur radio community. Compact, capable, and versatile — these rigs have been a staple for portable, QRP, and home station operators for decades. However, as Yaesu has discontinued this line, dedicated software support has all but disappeared with it. Existing tools are either outdated, fragmented across multiple applications, or simply never built with the FT-8XX operator in mind.

I saw that gap and decided to fill it.

As an FT-8XX operator myself, I found it frustrating that there was no single application that brought together everything I needed for a complete operating session. Switching bands meant navigating Yaesu's cumbersome built-in menu system. Voice transmission, digital modes, logging, and spotting all required separate tools that didn't talk to each other cleanly. I wanted one window, one workflow, and full control of my radio from my desk.

So I built it.

---

## What It Does

The Yaesu FT-8XX Suite connects to your radio via CAT control and gives you a clean, modern interface to operate from. Instead of digging through menus on the radio, everything is a click away:

**Voice TX** — Record your voice and play it back directly over the radio with a single button. PTT is keyed and released automatically, making it ideal for calling CQ or sending a standard message repeatedly without touching the mic.

**FT8 / FT4 Digital Modes** — Full integration with WSJT-X, launched and managed in the background so you never have to configure it separately. Work the digital bands without leaving the suite.

**Band and Mode Switching** — Change frequency, band, and mode instantly from the interface. No more scrolling through the Yaesu menu system to get where you need to go.

**DX and POTA Spotting** — Live spots from DX Cluster, POTA, and the Reverse Beacon Network pulled directly into the app. See what is active and click to tune straight to it.

**QSO Logging** — Log contacts as you make them and export to ADIF format for use with any logging software or award submission.

## Who Is It For?

This application is for any amateur radio operator running an FT-817, FT-818, FT-857 or FT-897 who wants a modern, capable control interface without the overhead of stitching together multiple tools. Whether you are a portable operator, a QRP enthusiast, or a home station operator who just wants things to work simply and reliably, this suite was built with you in mind.

## Installation

1. Download **YaesuFT8XXSuite_Setup_v2.1.0.exe** from above
2. Run the installer and follow the on-screen prompts
3. Launch the app from the desktop shortcut or Start Menu

That's it — no Python or extra tools required.

---

## Getting Started

Once the app is open, click the **Help** menu and select **Help Guide**. Everything you need to get up and running is covered there, including:

- Connecting your radio via CAT (serial port)
- Setting up your audio devices
- Using the voice recorder to record and transmit clips
- Running FT8 / FT4 digital modes via WSJT-X
- Logging QSOs and exporting to ADIF
- DX and POTA spotting

---

## Requirements

- Windows 10 or 11
- A Yaesu FT-817, FT-818, FT-857 or FT-897 radio
- A USB-to-serial CAT cable for your radio
- WSJT-X installed separately if you want digital modes
  - Download free from: https://wsjt.sourceforge.io

---

## Serial Port Access

If the app cannot open your CAT port, try running it as Administrator or check that your USB serial driver is installed correctly.
